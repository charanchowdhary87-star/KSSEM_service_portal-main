About KSSEM Service Portal

The KSSEM Service Portal is a digital platform designed to help students and staff report issues within the college more efficiently. Whether it's a maintenance request, security concern, transport issue, or a suggestion to improve campus facilities, users can quickly raise complaints without the need for login or disclosure of identity.

Key Features:

  • 🔐 Anonymous Complaint Submission: Report issues freely without revealing identity.
  • 📋 Category-Based Management: Complaints reach the correct department faster.
  • 🎯 Tracking System: Unique reference ID to monitor complaint status anytime.
  • ⚙️ Organized Workflow: Admins handle complaints via a secure and structured dashboard.

Safety & Misuse Policy:

To maintain a respectful, secure, and trustworthy environment, this portal includes strict monitoring systems. While anonymity is supported, misuse will not be tolerated.

Any attempt to:

  • ❌ Submit fake or misleading complaints
  • ❌ Defame individuals or departments
  • ❌ Use offensive or abusive language
  • ❌ Disrupt campus operations intentionally

Such actions may result in disciplinary measures. We securely log IP addresses, timestamps, and browser details to track misuse if required.

Our Mission:

To create a connected, responsive, and problem-free campus by simplifying the process of raising concerns and speeding up resolutions. Every voice matters — your feedback helps improve our campus for everyone.
